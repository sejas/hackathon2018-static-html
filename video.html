Silkom (Voice and Data) Limited offers an exciting range of IP based video cameras in the Axis range of equipment. Axis IP–Surveillance applications are used in Government, Retail, Banking, Education, and Industrial work places to provide monitoring and additional security to areas of risk. Axis video servers connect to existing analogue cameras, digitise the images and feed them to the IP network.

    Your images can be recorded to network DVRs and these Live or recorded video images can be viewed from anywhere over LAN, WAN, or Internet. These IP video cameras connect directly to your Cat5e/Cat6 cable system and can be viewed from any PC on the network.

    • High-quality images
    • Remote access and management
    • Integration with existing CCTC systems
    • Future-proof solutions
